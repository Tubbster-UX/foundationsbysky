About Me

"Many many years ago I was in a car accident and hurt my neck. I went to chiropractors that helped with the pain. I had 5 kids and before and in between and after my pain increased and lessened so me but never went away fully. I continued receiving chiropractic care, massage, crainiosacral therapy and some acupuncture. Each therapy helped some and kept me going so I could continue being the mom I wanted and needed to be. I was given a name by one of my therapists to try and see if they could help me get to the bottom of the pain I was continually having. She was a Structural Integration/Rolfer. After one session with her I never had the same intense pain again. I continued several more sessions with her to ensure we got to the bottom of the issue. Very soon after I was helped out of pain I made the decision I wanted to help others if I could and provide them with this amazing therapy also. So that brought me to becoming a massage therapist with plans to continue on with my learning to become a Structural Integration Therapist very soon. My goal when I started on this journey was to help people as I was helped by the many incredible people along my path.
